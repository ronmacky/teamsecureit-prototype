Top 10 SmartPhones Antivirus 2020

#1 360 Security



360Security is a bit of a mixed bag. Some of its security stuff isn’t half bad and then the other half is quite bad. The app scans your apps for potential malware activity, although its find rate is a little bit higher than we’re willing to believe.

✅ Scanning.
✅ Threat type.
✅ Lots of options and features
✅ Low system impact in the background
✅ Compatible in any devices
✅ Fans of easy-to-use friendly software



₱274.50




#2 Avira



Avira is one of the newer antivirus apps comparatively speaking. It grew rather quickly over the last year. The app comes with the basics, including device scans, real-time protection, external SD card scans, and a lot more.

✅ Real-Time Protection Against Threats
✅ Privacy scanning
✅ Blacklisting
✅ Automatic Malware Removal
✅ Keep Unwanted Apps and Attacks Away.
✅ Protect mobile devices from physical theft



₱599.00




#3 Avast Antivirus



Avast Mobile Securityis one of the most popular antivirus apps on any platform. It boasts over 100 million downloads and tons of features. Some of the features include the classic antivirus scanning, an applock, call blocker, anti-theft support, a photo vault, and even a firewall for rooted Android devices.

✅ Wi-Fi Inspector.
✅ Virus Chest
✅ Scans websites to protect your user data
✅ system files, encrypts connection
✅ Sensitive Data Shield
✅ Software Updater



₱149.50




#4 AVG Antivirus



AVG is another big name in the antivirus apps space. In fact, it’s basically the same as AVAST. AVAST actually purchased AVG back in 2016. Thus, the experience is similar in both antivirus apps.

✅ Real-time protection.
✅ Blacklisting.
✅ External storage device scanning, including SD cards.
✅ Privacy tracker that warns you whenever a hacker attempts
✅ Anti-theft
✅ Tracking the phone's location



₱149.00




#5 Bitdefender Antivirus



Bitdefender Antivirus is one of the few actually free antivirus apps. It hasn’t changed much over the years. It offers a basic scanning feature, a simple interface, quick performance, and no configuration.

✅ Best protection for your Android smartphone
✅ Secure VPN for a fast
✅ Remote locate
✅ Account Privacy that verifies whether your email account has been breached
✅ Minimal impact on battery life
✅ Lock and wipe your Android device in case of loss or theft



₱99.00




#6 Dr. Web Security Space



Dr.Web is one of the older antivirus apps and anti-malware apps. It has a decent set of features, including quick and full scans, protection from ransomware, a quarantine space, and even stats.

✅ Protects systems from Trojans and other malware
✅ Parental Control
✅ Anti-theft
✅ Anti-spam
✅ Firewall
✅ Security Auditor



₱399.99




#7 ESET Mobile Security and Antivirus



ESET is another big name in the antivirus and anti-malware world. It has a decent set of features, including scans, anti-theft support, a security auditor feature, scan scheduling, and more.

✅ App Lock
✅ Anti-Phishing
✅ Proactive Anti-Theft
✅ Scheduled Scanning
✅ Low Battery Alert
✅ Automatic update of virus database



₱99.50




#8 Kaspersky Mobile Antivirus



Kaspersky is one of the most popular antivirus apps. It has both a free and pro version. Both versions offer SMS and call blocking, scans, virus updates, and anti-theft. The premium version adds things like real-time protection, an applock, and more.

✅ Anti-theft features
✅ Multilayered protection
✅ Anti-malware
✅ Anti-spam
✅ Web application
✅ device controls



₱747.50




#9 Lookout Security & Antivirus



Lookout is another popular antivirus and anti-malware app. It comes pre-installed on many devices, especially on carriers like T-Mobile. It does the basics fairly well.

✅ Theft alerts
✅ Breach reports
✅ Identity protection
✅ GPS lost device location
✅ Photo backup
✅ Remote Lock



₱149.50

Malwarebytes is one of the most popular antivirus apps on Windows. The mobile version is pretty good too. It features an aggressively updated virus database, support for malware and ransomware, a permission tracker, and more.

✅ Detects ransomware before it can lock your device
✅ Safer browsing experience
✅ Conducts privacy audit for all apps
